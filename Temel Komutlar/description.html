Ingredients:
1 egg rather than the usual 2 eggs;
Brown sugar instead of white sugar;
Oil instead of butter;
Sour cream instead of milk;
Coffee to enhance chocolate flavour (optional);
Dutch-process cocoa powder for more intense chocolate flavour (optional);
Blooming the cocoa powder with hot milk;

How To Make it:
1. Sift dry ingredients: Combine flour, baking soda, and salt in a bowl. Sift cocoa powder separately.
2. Bloom cocoa: Mix coffee powder and hot milk with cocoa to enhance its flavor.
3. Finish batter: Whisk wet ingredients with cocoa, then add to dry ingredients. Whisk until smooth.
4. Stir in most of the chocolate chips, reserving some for topping.
5. Fill muffin cases: Use an ice cream scoop to fill cases 0.5cm from the rim. Top with reserved chocolate chips.
6. Bake at high temp for 5 minutes, then lower temp and bake for 20 minutes more until a toothpick comes out clean.
7. Cool briefly before handling as the muffins are fragile when hot.
